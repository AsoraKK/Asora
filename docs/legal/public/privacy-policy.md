# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-02-06  
Product: Lythaus (formerly Asora)

## 1. What we collect

- Account data: email address, authentication identifiers.
- Profile data: username, bio, avatar URL.
- Content data: posts, attached media URLs, moderation reports and appeals.
- Technical data: push notification token, app/device metadata, security signals.

## 2. Why we process data

- Provide core product features (feeds, posting, moderation, notifications).
- Enforce platform integrity and safety policy.
- Respond to legal obligations and abuse investigations.

## 3. Legal bases

- Contract performance for account and feature delivery.
- Legitimate interests for safety, abuse prevention, and reliability.
- Consent where required for optional analytics and marketing notifications.

## 4. Data retention

- Inactive accounts: target deletion after 24 months. First export user data to last known verified email, then delete after 30 days.
- User-deleted content: target purge within 30 days.
- Moderation cases: retained through closure plus 90 days.
- Operational logs: short retention with minimization controls.

## 5. Data subject rights

- Access/export and deletion requests are supported.
- Operational runbooks:
- `docs/runbooks/dsr.md`
- `docs/runbooks/dsr-settings.md`
